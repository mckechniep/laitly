03 · The Emblem

An apothecary seal for the editorial moments.

The emblem is an arched, dated wordmark with the droplet at its center and the brand promise running along the bottom curve. It's reserved for the moments that warrant ceremony — splash, packaging, editorial covers, the back of a business card.

Use it for

Splash, app store hero, packaging seal, editorial covers, business-card back, founder letter, slow-launch invitations.

Don't use it for

In-product chrome, tight UI surfaces, anything below ~120px, or contexts where the wordmark lockup already does the job.

Clear space

Reserve a margin equal to the height of the droplet on every side. Year and tagline are part of the mark — never crop them out.

04 · Color System

Warm cream. Soft taupe. A single shimmer of gold.

The palette stays close to skin and milk and morning light. Gold is the only chromatic accent — used sparingly for active states, progress, and the droplet itself. Every text-on-surface pair below clears WCAG AA.
