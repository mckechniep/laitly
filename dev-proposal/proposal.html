The Problem

Every mother is doing this math in her head.

1 in 3

breastfeeding mothers stop earlier than intended — not because they wanted to, but because the logistics overwhelmed them.

“How many oz do I have stored?”

Counted by hand, from memory.

“Will it expire before I use it?”

Guessed, then panicked.

“Do I have enough for work?”

Calculated at 3am.

“Should I pump again before I leave?”

No one tells her.

Laitly takes the mental load of breastfeeding off a mother’s plate — so she can just be present.

Every mother who chooses to breastfeed deserves support that actually works. Not a forum. Not a Google search at 3am. Laitly exists to do the milk math, track the movement, protect the supply, and plan for the moments away.

What We’re Building

Five features. Each one essential.

01

Milk Movement

Log every pump, bottle feed, and storage transfer in under 10 seconds.

02

Milk Math

Always know exactly how many oz are stored, where, and how long they’ll last.

03

Strategy

Plan for work, trips, and hours away with real oz targets based on baby’s intake.

04

Guides & FAQs

AAP-backed answers, how-to’s, and community-reviewed best practices in one place.

05

Me

Her profile, baby’s numbers, goals, reminders, and partner access — all controlled by her.
